To win this game, you must eat enough hamburgers to get to level 3 and have a score of 3. Along the way, you will level up and the game's difficulty will increase.
  • Don't forget to avoid the evil blue npc that turns red when it gets close to you.
  • You can go to the teleporters to be transported to a random other teleporter.
  • You can hit 'h' to reset your character's orientation and location.

The following things occur when you collide with these balls:

Pink ball:
Win Automatically!!
Rubbish:
-1 Health
Hamburger
+1 Score
Red Ball
+1 Health
